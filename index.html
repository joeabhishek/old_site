Implementation of efficient Arithmetic Units

The objective of this project was to develop a low power multiplier by architectural exploration. Emphasis was laid on dynamic power dissipation and swtiching activity.

The bypass technique in the architecture of carry save multiplier was used to reduce the power dissipation. The Wallace Tree Multiplier combined with the bypass technique helped increase the speed of hardware intensive operations and also reduce both dynamic and leakage power dissipations by 33%.
